Welcome to Nami support. Below you'll find answers to frequently asked questions and ways to contact us.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Nami work?

Open the app, tap a number to rate your mood, and you're done. Your mood scores are connected into a line graph so you can see the waves of your life over time.

Can I add notes to my mood?

Yes! After recording your mood score, you can optionally add a memo, emotion tags, a photo, or a voice memo to capture more context about how you're feeling.

What are emotion tags?

Tags let you label what you're feeling or what caused your mood (e.g., "happy", "stressed", "exercise", "sleep"). The app analyzes your tags to show patterns and insights in the statistics view.

Can I change the score range?

Yes! Go to Settings to change between 1-10, 1-30, or 1-100 scales. You can also switch between button and slider input modes.

About Premium Plans

You can purchase a monthly or yearly subscription, or a one-time ad removal from the Settings screen. Premium includes ad-free experience, unlimited custom tags, and automatic weather recording. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.

Is my data backed up?

Your mood data is automatically synced across devices via iCloud using the same Apple ID. Photos and voice memos are stored locally on your device and are included in your regular iPhone backups (iCloud or computer backups).

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual mood entries from within the app. You can also delete all data at once from the "Delete All Data" option in Settings. Alternatively, delete the app from your device to remove all data.

Does Nami work on Apple Watch?

Yes! The companion Apple Watch app lets you record your mood score and select tags right from your wrist. Data syncs to your iPhone automatically.
